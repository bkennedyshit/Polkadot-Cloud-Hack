# ReputeChain Demo Script (2-5 minutes)

## Scene 1: Problem Statement (30 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "Imagine you're a driver on Uber with a 4.9-star rating. You switch to Lyft. Your reputation? Gone. You start from zero. This happens to millions of gig workers every day."

**Visual:** Show screenshots of different platforms (Uber, Airbnb, Upwork) with different ratings for the same person.

---

## Scene 2: Solution Introduction (30 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "Introducing ReputeChain - the first decentralized reputation layer built on Polkadot. Your reputation is now portable, immutable, and owned by YOU."

**Visual:** Show the ReputeChain logo and architecture diagram.

---

## Scene 3: Wallet Connection (20 seconds)
**Action:** Click "Connect Wallet" button
**Visual:** Show wallet connection flow with Polkadot.js extension
**Voiceover:** "First, connect your wallet. ReputeChain works with any Polkadot-compatible wallet."

---

## Scene 4: Profile Creation (20 seconds)
**Action:** Profile automatically created after wallet connection
**Visual:** Show the Dashboard with empty profile
**Voiceover:** "Your reputation profile is instantly created on the blockchain. It's yours forever."

---

## Scene 5: Dashboard Overview (40 seconds)
**Action:** Scroll through the dashboard showing:
- Overall score (currently 0)
- Total reviews (0)
- Status (Active)
- Staked amount (0)
- Category breakdown (Communication, Reliability, Quality, Professionalism)

**Voiceover:** "The dashboard shows your complete reputation profile. Each category is tracked separately, giving you detailed insights into your performance."

---

## Scene 6: Submit a Rating (50 seconds)
**Action:** 
1. Enter a test address in "Rate User Address"
2. Set Overall Score to 5
3. Set all category ratings to 5
4. Click "Submit Rating"

**Voiceover:** "Let's submit a rating. I'll rate another user with a 5-star review. Notice how we can rate specific categories - communication, reliability, quality, and professionalism. This gives a much more nuanced view of reputation."

**Visual:** Show the transaction being submitted to the blockchain

---

## Scene 7: Leaderboard (30 seconds)
**Action:** Click on "Leaderboard" tab
**Visual:** Show the top performers with medals, rankings, and scores
**Voiceover:** "The global leaderboard shows the top-rated users across the entire network. Reputation is transparent and verifiable."

---

## Scene 8: Analytics (40 seconds)
**Action:** Click on "Analytics" tab
**Visual:** Show:
- Score trend chart (line graph showing improvement over time)
- Category performance (bar chart)
- Platform distribution (pie chart)
- Recent reviews

**Voiceover:** "Analytics give you deep insights into your reputation trends. Track your growth over time, see which categories need improvement, and review what others are saying about you."

---

## Scene 9: Key Benefits (40 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "ReputeChain offers three key benefits:

1. **Portability** - Your reputation follows you across all platforms
2. **Immutability** - No platform can delete or manipulate your history
3. **Ownership** - You control your reputation data, not a corporation"

**Visual:** Show icons/animations for each benefit

---

## Scene 10: Use Cases (30 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "ReputeChain works for:
- Ride-sharing drivers
- Tutors and educators
- Freelancers and contractors
- Hosts and property managers
- Any gig worker building trust"

**Visual:** Show quick clips of each use case

---

## Scene 11: Technical Stack (20 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "Built on Polkadot SDK with React frontend, IPFS storage, and full decentralization."

**Visual:** Show tech stack logos (Polkadot, React, IPFS)

---

## Scene 12: Call to Action (20 seconds)
**Voiceover:** "Join ReputeChain today. Build your portable reputation. Own your data."

**Visual:** Show website URL and social links

---

## Production Notes

### B-Roll Suggestions
- Gig workers in action (drivers, tutors, freelancers)
- Blockchain transactions being processed
- Dashboard animations and transitions
- Global map showing network growth

### Visual Style
- Modern, clean design
- Gradient colors (indigo to purple)
- Smooth animations
- Professional but approachable tone

### Audio
- Upbeat background music
- Clear voiceover
- Sound effects for button clicks and transactions

### Timing
- **2-minute version:** Scenes 1-6, 9, 12
- **5-minute version:** All scenes

### Recording Setup
- Screen recording at 1080p or 4K
- Good lighting for any talking head segments
- Professional microphone for voiceover
- Use Loom, OBS, or similar for screen recording
